Project of Leadership Training Center

DRCongo is experiencing a leadership crisis for many years, and the solution is to have good and educated servants leaders to respond to this crisis. Therefore, we want to create a leadership center focusing on the power of love and integrity. The goal is to educate more servants leaders who will apply the principles of love and integrity to create a just and beloved Congo for the realization of the common good and the greatest happiness of the people. The leadership center has two immediate goals. First, to educate new generation of leaders of integrity and help them develop and integrate four type of intelligences or capacities. One mental intelligence that implies vision for a better future. Two physical intelligence that implies discipline ,orderliness, and motivation . Three. Emotional intelligence implies self-control, passion, compassion, collaboration, and interdepency. Lastly, spiritual intelligence implies conscience, integrity, love, altruism , honesty generosity, and hospitality .
