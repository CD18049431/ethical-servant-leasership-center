Our Planned Reforms

Economic Reform

1. Fiscal Stability & Debt Management Implement responsible budgeting with a focus on reducing deficits. Streamline government spending by cutting waste and increasing efficiency. Improve tax collection to reduce evasion while keeping rates competitive. 2. Business Growth & Investment Simplify regulations to encourage entrepreneurship and attract foreign investment. Offer tax incentives for high-growth industries such as technology and manufacturing. Strengthen property rights and contract enforcement to boost investor confidence. 3. Job Creation & Workforce Development Invest in vocational training and digital skills programs to match labor market needs. Encourage private sector job growth through public-private partnerships. Support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) with access to credit and grants. 4. Infrastructure & Innovation Modernize transport, energy, and digital infrastructure to enhance productivity. Promote green energy initiatives to reduce dependency on fossil fuels. Foster research and development (R&D) through tax credits and innovation hubs. 5. Social Welfare & Cost of Living Implement targeted subsidies for essential goods to protect low-income families. Strengthen social security systems while ensuring financial sustainability. Promote affordable housing and healthcare reforms to reduce living costs. 6. Trade & Economic Diversification Reduce reliance on a single industry by diversifying into high-value sectors. Expand international trade partnerships to open new markets. Support agriculture and local industries to increase self-sufficiency and also agricultural, breeding,tourism,Road and digital infrastructure's investments, regulate land law.

Education

1. Access & Equity in Education Ensure universal access to quality education by expanding school infrastructure. Provide free or subsidized education for low-income families. Bridge the urban-rural education gap with targeted policies and digital learning. 2. Curriculum & Skill Development Modernize curricula to align with global standards and job market demands. Emphasize STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) and digital literacy. Promote vocational training and entrepreneurship education to reduce unemployment. 3. Teacher Training & Compensation Improve teacher training programs to enhance teaching quality. Offer competitive salaries and incentives to attract and retain skilled educators. Implement continuous professional development programs for teachers. 4. Higher Education & Research Increase funding for universities and research institutions. Strengthen partnerships between academia and industry to drive innovation. Provide scholarships and student loan programs for higher education affordability. 5. Technology Integration & Digital Learning

Healthcare

1. Universal Access & Affordability Expand healthcare coverage to ensure affordable access for all citizens. Strengthen public health insurance schemes to reduce out-of-pocket costs. Increase funding for primary healthcare centers, especially in rural areas. 2. Healthcare Workforce & Training Recruit, train, and retain more doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals. Improve working conditions and salaries to prevent brain drain. Expand medical education programs and offer incentives for service in underserved areas. 3. Hospital & Infrastructure Development Invest in modernizing hospitals and clinics with up-to-date medical technology. Strengthen emergency response systems and build more specialized medical centers. Implement digital health records and telemedicine to improve efficiency. 4. Preventive & Public Health Initiatives Promote vaccination campaigns and preventive care programs. Improve sanitation, clean water access, and nutrition programs to reduce disease burden. Strengthen awareness campaigns on lifestyle diseases such as diabetes and hypertension. 5. Pharmaceutical & Medical Supply Chain Regulate drug prices to make essential medicines more affordable. Encourage local pharmaceutical production to reduce dependence on imports. Ensure transparency and efficiency in medical procurement processes
